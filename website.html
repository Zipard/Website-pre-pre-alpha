Headings are for... headings. They go [h1] to [h6], larger to smaller.

Text can be made italicized or bold(ed) with [em] and [strong]

  • Ordered Lists and Unordered Lists are tagged with [ol] and [ul]
    • Then individual items are listed with [li]
  • The font can be changed with the style= {add on}

    • This deserves it's own section, as style= has multiple different attributes. They can be joined with a semicolon (ex: style="color: green; font-size: 32px") .

    • [style="color: green"]
    • [style="font-family: Impact"]
    • [style="font-size: 24px"]
